Definition of Derivative: $$f'(x)= {\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}}$$ Constant Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}(c)=0$$ Power Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(x^n\right)=nx^{n-1}$$ Product Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(f\cdot g\right)=f\cdot g' + g\cdot f'$$ Quotient Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)=\frac{g\cdot f' - f\cdot g'}{g^2}$$ Chain Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(f(g)\right)=f'(g)\cdot g'$$
