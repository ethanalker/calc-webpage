Definition of Derivative: $$f'(x)= {\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}}$$ Constant Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}(c)=0$$ Power Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(x^n\right)=nx^{n-1}$$ Product Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(f\cdot g\right)=f\cdot g' + g\cdot f'$$ Quotient Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)=\frac{g\cdot f' - f\cdot g'}{g^2}$$ Chain Rule: $$\frac{d}{dx}\left(f(g)\right)=f'(g)\cdot g'$$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{1}{x} \right)= \frac{-1}{x^2} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( e^x \right)= e^x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \ln{x} \right)= \frac{1}{x} $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sqrt{x} \right)= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( b^x \right)= b^x\ln b $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \log_b{x} \right)= \frac{1}{x\ln b} $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sin x \right)= \cos x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cos x \right)= -\sin x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \tan x \right)= \sec^2 x $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \csc x \right)= -\csc x \cot x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sec x \right)= \sec x \tan x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cot x \right)= -\csc^2 x $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sin^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{ \sqrt{1-x^2}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cos^{-1} x \right)= \frac{-1}{ \sqrt{1-x^2}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \tan^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{1+x^2} $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \csc^{-1} x \right)= \frac{-1}{x \sqrt{x^2-1}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sec^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{x \sqrt{x^2-1}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cot^{-1} x \right)= \frac{-1}{1+x^2} $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sinh x \right)= \cosh x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cosh x \right)= \sinh x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \tanh x \right)= \operatorname{sech}^2 x $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \operatorname{csch} x \right)= -\operatorname{csch}x \coth x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \operatorname{sech} x \right)= -\operatorname{sech}x \tanh x $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \coth x \right)= -\operatorname{csch}^2 x$$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \sinh^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{ \sqrt{x^2+1}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \cosh^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{ \sqrt{x^2-1}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \tanh^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{1-x^2} $$
$$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \operatorname{csch}^{-1} x \right)= \frac{-1}{ |x|\sqrt{1+x^2}} $$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \operatorname{sech}^{-1} x \right)= \frac{-1}{ x\sqrt{1-x^2}}$$ $$\frac{d}{dx} \left( \coth^{-1} x \right)= \frac{1}{1-x^2} $$
